Tutorial Visualization 1

Visualization 1 is a collapsible tree, also known as a node-link tree diagram.
General usage
If you click on a blue node, then the node becomes orange and it will show all of its children.
If you want more clarity, you can use the middle mouse button to zoom in and out. Pressing the left mouse button and dragging the figure around will move the graph accordingly.

Tutorial Visualization 2

Visualization 2 is a sunburst graph.
General usage
For the sunburst, only the first two layers are shown with the root node being the white one in the middle. Click on a node to go deeper into the tree. The clicked node now becomes the middle circle.
